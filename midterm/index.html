Life of Riley

Riley is a cockapoo and he is 5 years old. He was born September 2, 2016, and he arrived at his forever home on October 28, 2016. The trip to his new home was roughly 2 hours long and he was a very good boy; he whined for only a couple of minutes at the beginning of the trip and then he settled in and was asleep for the rest of the ride home. As a puppy, he would often be curious and mischievous, wanting to explore everything, as got himself into trouble sometimes. Riley had a few accidents in the house but we didn't get mad though because he was only a puppy, and it was very cold outside. Since then, his potty training went smoothly and he had very few accidents in the house. His favourite treats are cheese, dried liver bites, chicken, bell pepper, cucumber, lettuce, and broccoli; he is very healthy.
